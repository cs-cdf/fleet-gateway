# SWOT Analysis Template
# Usage: call("reasoning", template.replace("{{SUBJECT}}", subject).replace("{{CONTEXT}}", context))

Perform a comprehensive SWOT analysis for:

**Subject:** {{SUBJECT}}
**Context:** {{CONTEXT}}

---

## Strengths (internal, positive)
List 4-5 genuine strengths. Be specific — avoid generic statements like "good team".
For each: what is it, why it matters, evidence.

## Weaknesses (internal, negative)
List 4-5 honest weaknesses. Include things people often don't want to admit.
For each: what it is, root cause, impact.

## Opportunities (external, positive)
List 4-5 realistic external opportunities.
For each: what it is, timeframe, how to capitalize on it.

## Threats (external, negative)
List 4-5 genuine external threats.
For each: what it is, probability (high/medium/low), severity.
