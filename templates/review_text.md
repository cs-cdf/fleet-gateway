Evaluate as an expert editor/reviewer:

## Structure & Flow
- Is the organization logical? Does it flow naturally?
- Are transitions clear?
- Does the opening/conclusion work?

## Clarity & Precision
- Are there vague or ambiguous passages? (quote them)
- Is the language appropriate for the audience?
- Any jargon that needs explaining?

## Content & Completeness
- Is the main argument/message clear?
- What's missing that the reader would want to know?
- Any unsupported claims?

## Tone & Voice
- Is the tone consistent and appropriate?
- Any passages that feel off?

## Top 3 Improvements
Ranked by impact. Be specific and actionable.
