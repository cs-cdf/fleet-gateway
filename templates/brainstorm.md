# Brainstorm Template
# Usage: call("creative", template.replace("{{TOPIC}}", topic))

Brainstorm ideas for: **{{TOPIC}}**

Generate at least 10 distinct ideas. Mix:
- Obvious/conventional approaches (2-3)
- Unexpected/creative approaches (4-5)
- Radical/unconventional ideas (2-3)

Format each idea as:
**[n]. [Idea name]** — one sentence description. Why it could work.

Rules:
- Each idea must be genuinely different from the others
- No filler ("research more", "ask an expert")
- No disclaimers or hedging
- Quantity and variety matter more than perfection at this stage
- An idea that seems crazy can still be listed — filter later
